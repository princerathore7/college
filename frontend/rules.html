Acropolis — Rules & Regulations

General Rules

  • All students must carry their ID cards at all times.
  • Students must attend all scheduled classes punctually.
  • Respect teachers, staff, and fellow students at all times.
  • Maintain cleanliness in classrooms, corridors, and campus premises.
  • Mobile phones must be switched off or kept on silent during classes.

Attendance Rules

  • A minimum of 75% attendance is required to appear in exams.
  • Late arrivals and early departures will be recorded in attendance.
  • Medical leave must be submitted with proper documentation.
  • Unauthorized absence may lead to disciplinary action.

Academic Rules

  • Assignments and projects must be submitted by the given deadlines.
  • Plagiarism or copying is strictly prohibited.
  • Examination rules must be followed strictly.
  • Use of unfair means during exams will result in strict penalties.

Campus Conduct

  • Smoking, alcohol, or drug consumption is strictly prohibited on campus.
  • Wear proper dress code while on campus.
  • Follow library, lab, and computer lab regulations.
  • Participation in cultural and sports events is encouraged.

Safety & Security

  • Report any suspicious activity immediately to campus security.
  • Follow fire safety and emergency evacuation procedures.
  • Do not tamper with safety equipment or fire extinguishers.
  • Keep your belongings safe and do not leave valuables unattended.
